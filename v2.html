Questions we get asked

What types of roles does GTX specialise in?

We focus on senior and specialist roles — engineering (software, hardware, embedded), product, finance, operations, and life sciences. Roles where the global NRI talent pool significantly outperforms the local market.

How is GTX different from a regular recruitment agency?

We are exclusively NRI-focused. Our entire platform, events, and sourcing engine is built around NRI professionals who want to return to India. You won't find this talent on Naukri or LinkedIn India searches — these are people actively engaged with GTX.

What does it cost?

The initial consultation and talent briefing are completely free. GTX charges a placement fee only on a successful hire. We are outcome-aligned — we succeed when you make a great hire.

How quickly can I see candidates?

Most employers receive their first shortlist within 72 hours of submitting a brief. For niche or senior roles, it may take up to 5 business days to curate the right profiles.

Do NRI candidates need relocation support?

GTX provides relocation advisory resources and connects candidates with our partner network for housing, banking, and school admissions — reducing friction for both you and the hire.
