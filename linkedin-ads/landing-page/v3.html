Common questions

What's the cost structure?

The brief, consultation, and talent search are completely free. GTX charges a placement fee only when you successfully hire a candidate. We're outcome-aligned — we win when you make a great hire.

How quickly will I see candidates?

Most employers receive a first shortlist within 72 hours of submitting a complete brief. For senior or niche specialist roles, expect 5–7 business days.

What makes GTX different from LinkedIn or Naukri?

GTX is a curated NRI-specific network — not a search engine. Every candidate in our pool has been pre-screened for intent to return, relevant experience, and role-fit. You won't find these people through generic job boards.

What roles and industries do you cover?

Engineering, product, finance, operations, life sciences — primarily mid-to-senior roles where global NRI experience makes a meaningful difference. We've placed across technology, semiconductors, energy, pharma, and consulting.

Do you support relocation?

Yes. GTX provides candidates with relocation advisory resources and introductions to our partner network for housing, banking, and schools — making the return smoother for both the hire and your team.
